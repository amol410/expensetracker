1. Introduction

Expense Tracker is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

By using Expense Tracker, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

What You Provide:

  • Expense amounts, categories, descriptions, and dates

  • Budget settings

What We DON'T Collect:

  • ❌ Personal information (name, email, phone)

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used only for:

4. Data Storage

IMPORTANT: All your data is stored ONLY on your device.

We do not have servers. We cannot access your data. Your information never leaves your device unless you explicitly export and share it.

Storage Method:

  • SQLite database (local on your device)

  • No external servers

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your information with anyone. Period.

No Third-Party Services:

  • ❌ No analytics (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • ❌ No advertising networks
  • ❌ No crash reporting tools
  • ❌ No cloud storage services
  • ❌ No social media integrations

You have complete control over your data:
