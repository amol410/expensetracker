## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you use Expense Tracker, you may provide the following information:

- **Expense Data:** Amount, category, description, and date of expenses you manually enter
- **Currency Preference:** Your selected currency for displaying amounts
- **Budget Information:** Monthly budget limits you set (if any)
- **Categories:** Expense categories you select (Food, Transport, Shopping, Bills, Entertainment, Health, Others)

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

The App automatically collects the following local data:

- **Expense Records:** Transaction history stored locally on your device
- **App Preferences:** Settings such as selected currency, first launch status
- **Date/Time Information:** Timestamps for when expenses are created or modified

### 1.3 Information We DO NOT Collect

**Important:** Expense Tracker DOES NOT collect:

- ❌ Personal identifiable information (name, email, phone number, address)
- ❌ Financial account information (bank accounts, credit card numbers)
- ❌ Location data or GPS coordinates
- ❌ Device identifiers or advertising IDs
- ❌ Contacts or address book information
- ❌ Photos, camera, or microphone data (except when you choose to export data)
- ❌ Usage analytics or crash reports
- ❌ IP addresses or network information

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely for the following purposes:

### 2.1 Primary Functions

- **Expense Tracking:** To record and display your expenses
- **Budget Management:** To help you track spending against your budget
- **Statistics & Reports:** To generate spending insights and monthly reports
- **Currency Display:** To show amounts in your preferred currency
- **Data Export:** To allow you to export your data to CSV or PDF formats

### 2.2 App Functionality

- **Data Storage:** All your data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database
- **Preferences:** Save your settings (currency selection, first launch status) using SharedPreferences
- **Calculations:** Perform expense calculations, totals, and budget tracking locally

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Local Storage Only

**All your data is stored ONLY on your device.** We do not have servers, cloud storage, or any backend infrastructure. Your expense data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

**Storage Locations:**
- **Expense Data:** Stored in local SQLite database on your device
- **Preferences:** Stored in Android SharedPreferences on your device
- **Exported Files:** Saved to your device storage when you export to CSV/PDF

### 3.2 Security Measures

We implement the following security measures:

- **Local Encryption:** Data is protected by Android's built-in security features
- **No Network Transmission:** Your data is never transmitted over the internet
- **App Sandbox:** Data is isolated within the app's private storage area
- **Secure Storage:** Uses Android Room Database with built-in security
- **No Third-Party Access:** No third-party SDKs or services have access to your data

### 3.3 Your Responsibility

Since data is stored locally on your device:

- **Device Security:** Please secure your device with a PIN, password, or biometric lock
- **Backups:** We recommend regularly exporting your data for backup purposes
- **Device Loss:** If you lose your device, your expense data may be accessible to others if your device is not secured
- **App Uninstall:** Uninstalling the app will delete all your expense data permanently
